How it works

From invisible to recommended — in three steps

Most AI visibility tools show you a score. Jeevan AI tells you exactly why your competitor is appearing instead of you — and what to publish first to change it.

1

Run a scan

Enter your brand URL. Jeevan AI auto-discovers your brand details, competitors, and generates 25 buyer queries across all five AI platforms. See exactly where your brand appears — and where it doesn't.

2

See the gap

Every gap is scored on the specific buying factor causing it — Trust, Use Case Fit, Pricing, Ease of Use, or Quality. You see which competitor is benefiting and exactly what content they have that you don't.

A 4-week content calendar is generated from your gap analysis — grounded in your Knowledge Base and social signal data. Every brief is specific enough for a writer to execute without asking a single question.
