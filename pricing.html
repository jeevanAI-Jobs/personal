Pricing FAQ

How much does Jeevan AI cost?

Jeevan AI has a free forever plan at $0, and three paid plans billed monthly: Starter at $49 per month, Pro at $149 per month, and Agency at $399 per month. All paid plans are billed in US dollars.

Is there a free version of Jeevan AI?

Yes. The Free plan is free forever and requires no credit card. It scans 5 buying queries across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, gives you a buying factor score breakdown, identifies your top 3 gaps, and covers 1 brand.

What is the difference between the Starter, Pro, and Agency plans?

Starter at $49 per month covers 25 queries across all 5 AI platforms, full gap analysis, a 4-week content calendar, brief generation, and re-scan tracking for 1 brand. Pro at $149 per month adds the Social Intelligence feed, a Knowledge Base, intelligence chat, competitor monitoring, weekly digests, and 3 brands. Agency at $399 per month adds unlimited brands, white-label reports, client-level dashboards, priority support, an onboarding call, and API access.

Is there an early-access discount?

Yes. The first 50 paid customers get free upgrades for 12 months on any plan.
