Social Intelligence

The external signals that decide how AI describes you

AI engines build their answers mostly from sources outside your own website. Social Intelligence tracks those external signals in two streams, so you can see what is actually shaping your AI perception.

👽

Reddit Intelligence

Reddit is the single most cited domain across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. Reddit Intelligence tracks the threads, subreddits, and sentiment shaping your category, shows where competitors are being discussed and you are not, and flags the conversations AI is pulling from to describe you.

🔍

Google Intelligence

Google still feeds the AI layer, from AI Overviews to Gemini. Google Intelligence tracks how your brand surfaces across Google's search and AI experiences, and which pages and sources Google is associating with your brand, so you can see the link between your search presence and your AI presence.

Why Reddit matters more than most teams realise

Reddit now accounts for a larger share of AI citations than any other website. If your category lives on Reddit and your brand does not, AI has a one-sided story to tell. We explain the data and what to do about it on a dedicated page.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What does Jeevan AI do?

Jeevan AI scans your brand across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI Mode, scores it on the five buying factors AI uses to decide which brand to recommend, identifies which factor is causing each gap and which competitor benefits, and generates a prioritized content plan to close those gaps.

Which AI platforms does Jeevan AI track?

Jeevan AI tracks five platforms: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI Mode. It reports a separate visibility score for each so you can see where you are strong and where a competitor is being recommended instead.

What is Social Intelligence in Jeevan AI?

Social Intelligence surfaces the external signals AI engines rely on to describe your brand. It has two streams: Reddit Intelligence, which tracks community threads and sentiment because Reddit is the single most cited domain across AI answers, and Google Intelligence, which tracks how your brand surfaces in Google's search and AI experiences. Together they show which third-party sources are shaping your AI perception.

How is Jeevan AI different from other AI visibility tools?

Most tools tell you whether you appeared and give you a score. Jeevan AI tells you why you were skipped by scoring the specific buying factor causing each gap, then turns that into a writer-ready content plan. It is built to produce a next action, not just a dashboard.
