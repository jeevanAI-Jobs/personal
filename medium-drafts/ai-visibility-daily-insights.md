Most AI visibility platforms produce dashboards: citation frequency went up 3%, mention rate is flat, share of voice against three competitors. These are metrics, not insights. A useful daily insight tells you specifically what changed, which buying factor it affects, and what to do about it today — in one sentence, without requiring the reader to interpret trend lines or compare data points across multiple views.

But it produces a platform that is comprehensive and largely unused — because brand managers do not have the time to become GEO analysts in addition to their existing responsibilities.
