5 Best Profound Alternatives in 2026

Profound is a powerful platform, but its enterprise pricing and analytics depth are more than many B2B teams need. Here are five honest alternatives, including some we do not make, compared on price, focus, and fit.

Why Look for a Profound Alternative?

Profound is the most analytically deep AI visibility platform in the category, and for enterprise teams with dedicated analysts, that depth is genuinely valuable. But it is also the enterprise option: generally the highest priced in the category and sold through sales-led contracts. Smaller and mid-sized B2B teams often find it is more data, and more cost, than they actually need.

The most common reasons teams look elsewhere: they want accessible self-serve pricing, a free way to start, and clearer guidance on what to do next rather than a dashboard of raw analytics to interpret themselves. Here are the alternatives worth considering.

#3 · Lowest-cost entry

Otterly.ai

Lite tier around $29/month

Otterly.ai is the cheapest credible way to start tracking, with a Lite tier around $29 a month covering a limited set of prompts across a few engines. It is a good entry point for very small teams or for validating whether AI visibility tracking is worth investing in, before moving to something more capable. Coverage is limited at the entry price, so it suits narrow monitoring rather than a full program.

Pricing: ~$29/moFocus: Budget prompt tracking
#4 · The incumbent itself

Profound

Enterprise pricing

Worth stating plainly: if you are an enterprise with a dedicated analytics team and the budget to match, Profound remains the deepest platform and may be the right answer. The reason to consider alternatives is fit, not quality. Profound is excellent at what it does; it is simply built for a different, larger buyer than most B2B SaaS teams.

Pricing: EnterpriseFocus: Deep analytics
#5 · The free baseline

Manual DIY Tracking

Free (your time)

You can track AI visibility manually by running a consistent set of buyer queries across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini each month and recording whether your brand appears. It is free and fine for very early validation, but it does not scale, lacks history, and consumes time that compounds quickly. Most teams outgrow it within a month or two.
