The 6 Best AI Visibility Tools for 2026

A practitioner comparison of the tools that track whether AI engines recommend your brand, from free scans to enterprise analytics. We make one of these, and we have tried to be honest about where each one actually wins.

What an AI Visibility Tool Should Do

Before the list, the criteria. A genuinely useful AI visibility tool should track whether and how your brand appears across the major AI engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini), show which competitors appear when you do not, reveal which sources AI cites in your category, and ideally translate all of that into specific actions. The best ones also track sentiment and change over time. Pure tracking tells you where you stand; the most useful tools tell you what to do about it. We ranked these tools against that standard.

How to Choose the Right One

Start by naming your actual constraint. If it is analytics depth and you have the budget, Profound. If it is clean reporting, Peec. If it is cost, Otterly or a regional tool. If it is knowing what to do next without hiring a strategist, Jeevan AI. Most teams discover their real constraint is the last one, which is why we built the tool the way we did. Whatever you choose, start by establishing a baseline, then track change over time, because measuring the right KPIs matters more than the specific tool.
