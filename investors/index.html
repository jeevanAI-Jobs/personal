Search is shifting. Brands are not ready.

AI search adoption is compressing a multi-year window into months. ChatGPT reached 100 million users in two months. Perplexity is growing faster than any search engine in history. Google AI Mode has changed how billions of people interact with search results.

Brands that build AI visibility now will have a compounding advantage over those that wait. A brand that waits until 2027 or 2028 is starting a race where the leaders have a year of compounded advantage.

The GEO category is where SEO was in 2005. The brands and tools that define it now will be the category leaders when it becomes mainstream. Jeevan AI is building to be that tool.
