The key difference: diagnosis vs monitoring

Most AI visibility tools tell you your citation score — how often your brand appears in AI answers. That is useful for tracking progress. What they do not tell you is why your competitor appears instead of you and exactly what content will change that. Jeevan AI is built around the diagnostic layer: scoring your brand against the buying factors AI uses to make recommendations, and generating the specific content brief that closes the identified gap.
