Jeevan AI vs Peec AI

Both track your share of voice across AI engines at a mid-market price. The difference is what happens after the tracking: pure reporting, or reporting plus a prioritized plan to improve. Here is the honest comparison.

The Short Version

Peec AI and Jeevan AI compete in the same mid-market segment, and both are good at what they do. Peec AI is a clean, capable tracker for monitoring share of voice across AI engines, with solid reporting for teams, priced around $99 a month. Jeevan AI tracks the same engines but is built to go one step further: it translates the data into a prioritized recommendation of what to fix next, and it offers a free scan so you can see where you stand before committing.

The choice comes down to a simple question: do you want a tool that reports your AI visibility clearly, or a tool that reports it and tells you what to do about it?

Where Peec AI Wins

Peec AI is a clean, well-built share-of-voice tracker. If your primary need is reliable reporting on where your brand appears across AI engines, presented in a way that is easy for a team to read and share, Peec AI does that job well at a reasonable mid-market price. For teams that already know their content strategy and just want clear measurement to track progress, Peec AI is a solid, focused choice.

Where Jeevan AI Wins

Jeevan AI's differentiator is what happens after the measurement. Rather than leaving you to interpret a share-of-voice report and decide what to do, it surfaces the single highest-priority action to improve your visibility. For teams that do not have a dedicated GEO strategist, that translation from data to action is the difference between knowing you have a problem and knowing how to fix it.

It also starts with a free scan, so you can assess your current AI visibility before paying anything. The philosophy behind this is something we have written about directly: most teams do not need another dashboard, they need one clear thing to do next. Jeevan AI is built around that.

The honest summary: if you want clean tracking and you will handle strategy yourself, Peec AI is a good fit. If you want the tracking plus a prioritized plan and a free way to start, Jeevan AI goes further for a similar or lower cost.

How to Choose

  • Choose Peec AI if: you want straightforward, clean share-of-voice reporting and you already have a content strategy you are executing.
  • Choose Jeevan AI if: you want tracking plus a prioritized recommendation of what to fix next, and you want to start with a free scan.
