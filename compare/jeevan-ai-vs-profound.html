Jeevan AI vs Profound

Both track your brand across AI engines. One is built for enterprise analysts who want maximum data depth. The other is built for teams that want to know what to fix next. Here is the honest comparison.

The Short Version

Profound and Jeevan AI are both legitimate AI visibility tools, but they are built for different buyers. Profound is the deep analytics platform: it tracks brand mentions and citations across the major AI engines, offers extensive data exploration, and is priced and sold for enterprise teams that have analysts to interpret it. Jeevan AI tracks the same engines but is built around a different promise: instead of handing you a dashboard full of numbers, it tells you the single most important thing to fix next, and it starts with a free scan.

If you have a dedicated analytics function and an enterprise budget, Profound's depth is genuinely valuable. If you are a small or mid-sized B2B team that wants to improve AI visibility without hiring an analyst to read the dashboard, Jeevan AI is the more practical choice.

Where Profound Wins

Profound is the most analytically deep option in the category. It tracks brand mentions and citations across the major engines, supports detailed data exploration, and is designed for teams that want to draw their own conclusions from granular data. For an enterprise with a marketing analytics team, a large content operation, and the budget to match, Profound's depth is a real advantage. If your organization treats AI visibility as a dedicated analytics discipline with people whose job is to interpret dashboards, Profound rewards that investment.

Where Jeevan AI Wins

Jeevan AI is built on a different belief: most teams do not need more data, they need to know what to do. It tracks your brand across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini and then surfaces the single highest-priority action to improve your visibility, rather than leaving you to figure it out from a dashboard. It starts with a free scan, so you can see where you stand before paying anything, and its pricing is built for teams without an enterprise contract.

This is the practical edge for most B2B SaaS teams. As we documented in our zero-budget case study, the work that actually moves AI visibility is focused, prioritized execution, not data volume. Jeevan AI is designed to point you at that work directly.

The honest summary: Profound gives you more and asks more of you. Jeevan AI gives you a clear next step and a free way to start. Neither is wrong, they serve different teams. Choose based on whether your constraint is data depth or time to act.

How to Choose

  • Choose Profound if: you are an enterprise with a dedicated analytics team, you want maximum data granularity, and budget is not the primary constraint.
  • Choose Jeevan AI if: you are a small or mid-sized B2B team, you want to know what to fix next without interpreting a dashboard, and you want to start free.
