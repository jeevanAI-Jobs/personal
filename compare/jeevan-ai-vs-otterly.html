The Short Version

Otterly.ai and Jeevan AI sit at the accessible end of the AI visibility market, but they aim at slightly different buyers. Otterly.ai is the cheapest credible way to start, with a Lite tier around $29 a month covering a limited set of prompts across a few engines. It is a clean, budget-first prompt tracker. Jeevan AI tracks across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini and adds a prioritized content action plan, starting with a free scan so you can see where you stand before paying.

If your only goal is to watch a handful of prompts at the lowest possible cost, Otterly is a sensible choice. If you want broader engine coverage and guidance on what to fix, Jeevan AI offers more for teams ready to act.

Where Otterly.ai Wins

Otterly.ai's advantage is simple and real: price. At around $29 a month, it is the cheapest credible way to begin tracking your brand across AI engines. For a solo founder, a very small team, or anyone who just wants to validate whether AI visibility tracking is worth deeper investment, that low entry cost is genuinely useful. If you want to watch a small set of prompts without committing budget, Otterly does that job at the lowest price in the category.

Where Jeevan AI Wins

Jeevan AI's advantage is coverage plus guidance. It tracks across all four major engines and, rather than handing you tracking numbers, surfaces the single highest-priority action to improve your visibility. It also starts with a free scan, so the entry cost is effectively zero to see where you stand. For teams that want to actually improve their AI visibility rather than just monitor it, that combination of broader coverage and a clear next step does more than a minimal prompt tracker. The reasoning behind this action-first approach is in our piece on why one clear action beats another dashboard.

The honest summary: Otterly wins on lowest absolute price for minimal monitoring. Jeevan AI wins on coverage and actionable guidance, and it also lets you start free. Choose Otterly if cost is the only constraint; choose Jeevan AI if you want to improve, not just watch.

How to Choose

  • Choose Otterly.ai if: you want the cheapest possible way to track a few prompts and budget is the deciding factor.
  • Choose Jeevan AI if: you want broader engine coverage, a prioritized plan for what to fix, and a free way to start.
