What the full audit covers

Six areas that together determine whether AI recommends you or your competitor.

Area 01

AI platform scan

We run 15-20 real buyer queries across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. We record exactly what each engine says about your brand, your competitors, and your category.

Area 02

Competitor gap analysis

We run the same queries for your competitors and map where they appear and you don't — and why. Most brands find 2-3 specific gaps driving nearly all the difference.

Area 03

Reddit and social presence

We scan Reddit and social for brand mentions, sentiment, and the specific threads that AI engines are most likely to cite when answering questions in your category.

Area 04

Page structure and schema

We check your homepage and key pages for AI-readability signals: structured data, self-contained answer blocks, entity definitions, and FAQ coverage.

Area 05

Brand entity consistency

AI engines build a knowledge graph of your brand. We check whether your definition is consistent and extractable across your website, G2, LinkedIn, and Reddit.

Area 06

Prioritized action plan

Not a list of 40 things to fix. We give you the 3-5 highest-leverage actions specific to your situation, in order of impact, with enough detail to act on immediately.
