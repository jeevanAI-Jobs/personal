Why generate schema from the real page?

  
Most schema generators give you an empty form to fill in by hand. This one reads your live page and fills the fields from your actual content, the right types, the real values, so the structured data matches what the page says. Schema markup is one of the proven levers for AI search visibility because it gives engines machine-readable entity data.

  
What to do next

  
Paste the generated JSON-LD inside a <script type="application/ld+json"> tag in your page's <head>, make sure any FAQ text matches the visible page, and validate with a structured-data testing tool. Then run the page through the AI-Readiness Page Analyzer to confirm the score moved.
