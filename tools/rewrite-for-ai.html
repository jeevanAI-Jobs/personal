What changed

    Why rewrite for AI?

    AI engines cite self-contained passages of roughly 50-150 words that lead with a direct answer and make specific, verifiable claims, this structure gets cited about 2.3x more often than flowing, context-dependent prose. This tool applies that format to your text without changing the facts. The full principle is in our guide to the content formats AI search cites and writing content AI will actually cite.

    How to use the result

    Paste the rewritten version back into your page, then check the whole page with the AI-Readiness Page Analyzer. For FAQ-style content, pair it with the AI Schema Generator so the question/answer pairs are also machine-readable.
