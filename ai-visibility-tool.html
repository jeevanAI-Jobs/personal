What is an AI visibility tool?

Traditional SEO tools track Google rankings. AI visibility tools track whether AI recommends you.

When a buyer asks ChatGPT “what’s the best CRM for startups” or asks Gemini “best moisturiser for dry skin in Dubai,” AI platforms evaluate every brand on specific buying decision factors before generating a recommendation. An AI visibility tool measures where your brand appears (and where it doesn’t) across these AI platforms — and tells you exactly why you’re being skipped.

This matters because 51% of B2B buyers now start vendor research in an AI chatbot, not Google. If your brand is invisible in AI responses, you’re losing deals to competitors who have the content infrastructure AI systems need to cite and recommend them.

Jeevan AI is the AI visibility tool built to solve this. It doesn’t just tell you whether you appeared — it tells you which buying factor is causing you to be skipped, which competitor is benefiting, and what content to publish first to change it. Frequently asked questions

Common questions about AI visibility tools

What is an AI visibility tool?

An AI visibility tool scans your brand across AI platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Mode to measure how often and how favourably your brand is recommended. Unlike traditional SEO tools that track Google rankings, AI visibility tools measure citation presence in generative AI responses — where an increasing share of buyer research now happens.

How does Jeevan AI’s AI visibility tool work?

Three steps: (1) Enter your brand URL — the tool auto-discovers your brand, competitors, and generates 25 buyer queries; (2) The scanner runs these queries across 5 AI platforms, scoring your brand on the 5 buying decision factors; (3) You get a gap analysis and a 4-week content plan to fix each gap.

How is an AI visibility tool different from an SEO tool?

SEO tools measure Google search rankings. AI visibility tools measure whether AI platforms cite and recommend your brand when buyers ask questions in ChatGPT or Gemini. A brand can rank #1 on Google but be completely invisible in AI recommendations because AI evaluates different signals: structured content, third-party validation, and use-case specificity rather than backlinks and keyword density.
