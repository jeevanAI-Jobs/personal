Background

Building AI Systems from the Inside Out

Shiv has spent over a decade inside the organizations that defined how people find information online. At Google, he worked on core search infrastructure, developing a firsthand understanding of how ranking signals are constructed, how entity knowledge graphs are built, and why some sources get cited while others disappear. That understanding of how machines decide what is credible is the foundation everything at Jeevan AI is built on.

At Microsoft, Shiv contributed to AI product development at a time when language models were moving from research curiosity to production systems. He was part of teams building the inference, retrieval, and grounding pipelines that power today's AI answers at scale. He understands not just what AI platforms return, but why: the architecture decisions, the data weighting choices, the trust signals that determine which sources make it into a model's output and which do not.

At Meta, Shiv worked on AI-driven content and recommendation systems at a scale that few organizations in the world operate at. Understanding how billions of pieces of content get ranked, recommended, and surfaced to users trained a discipline around measurability and signal clarity that now shapes how Jeevan AI approaches brand visibility tracking.

Why Jeevan AI

The Problem Only an Insider Could See

Most of the people building AI visibility tools are SEO practitioners who watched their traffic drop and looked for a new framework. Shiv came at it from the other direction. He had been inside the systems generating AI answers, and he could see exactly what brands were getting wrong because he knew what those systems were actually looking for.

The gap between what AI platforms reward and what most brand marketing teams produce is wide and growing. AI platforms cite sources with specific, verifiable, consistently-stated claims across multiple independent touchpoints. Most brand content is promotional, platform-siloed, and structured for human readers rather than machine extraction. Jeevan AI exists to close that gap: to give brand teams the visibility data they need to understand where they stand, and the guidance to build citation assets that actually work.

The window is real. The brands that build AI visibility now will hold structural citation advantages for years. Shiv built Jeevan AI because he knew from the inside that this window would not stay open, and that most brands would miss it without something telling them exactly what to do.
