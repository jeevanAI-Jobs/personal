Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Jeevan AI ("we", "our", or "us") operates jeevanai.co.in and the Jeevan AI dashboard at dashboard.jeevanai.co.in. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Create an account or sign up for a free scan
  • Enter a brand name or URL for analysis
  • Contact us by email

We also collect usage data automatically, including pages visited, time on site, and browser type, through Google Analytics (GA4). This data is anonymized and aggregated.

How we use your information

  • To provide and improve the Jeevan AI platform and reports
  • To send product updates or responses to your enquiries
  • To understand how users interact with the site so we can improve it

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising targeting.

Cookies

We use essential cookies required for the platform to function, and analytics cookies via Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly without them.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption and access controls. We retain account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Third-party services

We use Google Analytics for usage analytics and Google Tag Manager. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not control how Google processes data collected through these tools.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email us at jeevanaijobs@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes means you accept the updated policy.
